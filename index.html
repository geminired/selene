SELENE PROGRAM

OUR MISSION

During the Selene Program, our crew will explore the Moon's environment and resources to develop the habitation systems needed for future human exploration. In alignment with NASA's Artemis missions, the Selene Program will effectively collect data, maintain an economic mission plan, and ensure a high scientific return so that we can eventually reach our next goal: Mars.

MISSION PATCH

Our mission patch represents our ultimate journey from the Earth to the Moon creating this habitable environment to help explore into deep space such as Mars. Selene, often identified with Artemis, is the goddess of the moon who symbolizes the moonlight that reaches Earth.
